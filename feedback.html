Send Feedback

We love hearing from our users! Whether you have a suggestion, compliment, or general feedback, we're all ears.

What Happens to Your Feedback

We read every piece of feedback we receive. Here's what happens after you submit:

  1. Review: Our team reviews all feedback within 48 hours
  2. Categorization: We categorize feedback by type and priority
  3. Planning: Feature suggestions are considered for future updates
  4. Implementation: Top-voted features get added to our development roadmap

Recent Feedback Implemented

  • Dark Mode: Added in v2.0 based on user requests
  • Export to PDF: Implemented in v2.1 after numerous requests
  • Biometric Lock: Added fingerprint/face recognition in v1.5
