Account Deletion

We're sorry to see you go. This page explains how to delete your NotesNest account and what happens when you do.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly from the app:

  1. Open NotesNest on your device
  2. Go to Settings > Account
  3. Tap on "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision by entering your password
  5. Tap "Permanently Delete Account"

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing us at support@notesnest.com from the email address associated with your account.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

When you delete your account:

  • All your notes, notebooks, and tags are permanently deleted from our servers
  • Your account information is removed from our systems
  • Any active subscriptions will be canceled

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please download any important notes before proceeding.

Data Retention

After account deletion, we may retain certain information for legitimate business purposes or to comply with legal obligations, but this information will no longer be associated with your identity.

Need Help?

If you're having trouble deleting your account or have questions about the process, please contact our support team for assistance.
