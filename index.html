Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NotesNest

Is NotesNest free to use?

Yes, NotesNest is free to download and use with basic features. We offer a premium subscription with additional features like advanced formatting, cloud storage, and priority support.

How do I sync my notes across devices?

Simply create an account with your email address, and your notes will automatically sync across all devices where you're logged in. You can enable or disable sync in the app settings.

Is my data secure with NotesNest?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption to ensure your notes remain private. We also offer biometric authentication (fingerprint and face recognition) to protect access to your notes.
