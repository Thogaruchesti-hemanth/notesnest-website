Technical Support

Experiencing issues with NoteNest? For faster resolution, please include the following details in your email:

  • Your device model and Android version
  • NoteNest app version (found in Settings > About)
  • Detailed description of the issue you're experiencing
  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • Screenshots or error messages (if applicable)
  • Whether the issue occurs with reminders, notes, backups, widgets, or other features

Common Issues

Before contacting support, check if your issue matches these common scenarios:

  • Reminders not working: Some devices (Xiaomi MIUI, Vivo, Oppo) have aggressive battery optimization. Disable battery optimization for NoteNest in your device settings.
  • Widget not updating: Some OEM launchers may delay widget updates. Try removing and re-adding the widget.
  • Backup issues: Ensure you have sufficient storage space and necessary permissions enabled.
  • Google Sign-In delays: First-time token refresh may take a moment. Wait 10-15 seconds and try again.
