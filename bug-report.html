Report a Bug

Found an issue in NoteNest? We appreciate you taking the time to report it! Your bug reports help us maintain app stability and improve the experience for all users. We actively monitor all reports via Firebase Crashlytics, but detailed reports from users like you help us fix issues faster.

Before Reporting: Check our Known Issues section below to see if your issue is already being addressed. For general questions or support, please visit our Contact page.

Screenshots, screen recordings, or error messages help us understand and fix the issue faster. You can attach multiple files.

What Happens After You Report?

  1. Acknowledgment: You'll receive an automated confirmation email
  2. Triage: Our team reviews and prioritizes the bug within 24-48 hours
  3. Investigation: We reproduce and analyze the issue using Firebase Crashlytics data
  4. Fix: Critical bugs are addressed in emergency patches; others in scheduled updates
  5. Update: We notify you when the fix is released (if you provided email)

Known Issues

We're already aware of these issues and working on fixes:

  • Reminders not triggering on certain devices High Priority

    Affected Devices: Xiaomi (MIUI), Vivo, Oppo, Realme with aggressive battery optimization

    Workaround: Go to Settings > Battery > App Battery Management > Find NoteNest > Disable battery optimization or enable "Autostart"

    Status: In Progress - Working on enhanced background task handling

  • Widget update delays on some launchers Medium Priority

    Affected: Some OEM launchers delay widget updates

    Workaround: Remove and re-add the widget, or force-refresh by opening the app

    Status: Under Investigation

  • Google Sign-In token refresh delay Low Priority

    Issue: First-time Google Sign-In may take 10-15 seconds

    Workaround: Wait a moment and try again

    Status: Known limitation - Google API behavior

  • Large image exports may freeze on low-end devices Medium Priority

    Affected: Devices with less than 3GB RAM when exporting notes with many images

    Workaround: Export as text or PDF instead of image format

    Status: Planned for optimization in v3.1.0

  • Tablet landscape mode UI refinement needed Low Priority

    Issue: Some UI elements not fully optimized for tablet landscape orientation

    Status: Scheduled for v3.1.0

Crash Reporting & Privacy

NoteNest uses Firebase Crashlytics to automatically collect crash reports. This helps us identify and fix issues quickly. Crash reports include:

  • Device model and Android version
  • App version and crash stack trace
  • Device state at time of crash (memory, network status)

Privacy Note: Crash reports do NOT include your notes content, personal information, or any identifiable data. You can disable crash reporting in Settings > Privacy > Crash Reporting. For more information, see our Privacy Policy.

Alternative Reporting Methods

Email: Send bug reports to saihemanth225@gmail.com with subject "NoteNest Bug Report"

Play Store Review: Report bugs in your Google Play review (though we prefer this form for detailed tracking)

In-App: Use Settings > Support > Report Bug within NoteNest

Thank You for Helping Us Improve!

Every bug report makes NoteNest more stable and reliable for everyone. We're committed to maintaining a high-quality, crash-free experience. Thank you for your patience and for taking the time to report issues!
